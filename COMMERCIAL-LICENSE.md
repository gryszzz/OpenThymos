This file is a business template for your framework product and is not legal advice. Replace it with counsel-reviewed terms before launch.

All source code, design assets, documentation, and bundled materials in this product remain the intellectual property of the seller unless a separate written agreement says otherwise.

## 2. License grant

Upon purchase, the buyer receives a non-exclusive, non-transferable commercial license to:

- use the product internally
- modify the source code for their own business or client work
- deploy derivative applications built from the framework

## 3. Restrictions

The buyer may not:

- resell, sublicense, or redistribute the framework as a competing template, boilerplate, or framework
- share repository access with parties outside the licensed team
- remove copyright or attribution notices where they are required by bundled third-party licenses

## 4. Client work

Agency or studio buyers may use the product in client projects only if their purchased tier explicitly permits client delivery.

## 5. Support and updates

Support scope, update windows, onboarding help, and refund terms should be described on the order form or commercial agreement associated with the purchase.

## 6. Third-party services

The buyer is responsible for their own accounts, fees, and compliance for hosting providers, model providers, and external APIs they choose to use.

## 7. Warranty disclaimer

The product is provided "as is" without warranties of any kind, to the maximum extent permitted by law.

## 8. Liability cap

To the maximum extent permitted by law, the seller's total liability arising from the product will not exceed the amount paid by the buyer for the applicable license.

## 9. Termination

The license terminates automatically if the buyer breaches these terms. Upon termination, the buyer must stop distributing the framework itself, though already-deployed end products may continue only if allowed by local law and your final commercial terms.
